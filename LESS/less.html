How Do We Define E-Learning?

An e-learning course could be a software simulation that demonstrates the click path through an application. Or,it could be a very interactive course that features role playing and complex decision-making. In this article, we will take a deliberately broad view E-Learning , and appreciate its near-infinite and ever-evolving forms.
